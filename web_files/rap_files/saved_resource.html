CHAMORITA RESORT

CHAMORITA RESORT

is located in Biliran Province Central Philippine providing comfortable and safe accomodations and amenities for visitors to fit all budgets. It is conveniently right off the national highway in Barangay Catmon on the shores of Biliran Straits between the town of Biliran and the Provincial Capital of Naval. Chamorita provides a place to stay while visiting friends or family or while touring the many interesting sights on the beautiful island of Biliran.

ROOMS
